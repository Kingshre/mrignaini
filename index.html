200+ Years of Heritage
The Mrignaini Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Woven for Today

Born in the vibrant lanes of Jaipur, Mrignaini is a celebration of India's timeless block printing art. Each piece tells a story — carved by master artisans whose families have practised this craft for generations.

We work hand-in-hand with local craftsmen, using natural dyes sourced from indigo, pomegranate, and marigold to create fabrics that breathe tradition and sustainability.

The Art of Creation

How Our Fabrics Come to Life

01

Design & Carving

Master artisans hand-carve intricate motifs into teak wood blocks — each block a unique work of art taking days to perfect.

02

Natural Dyeing

Colours are extracted from nature — indigo for blues, pomegranate for yellows, madder root for reds — mixed to our heritage recipes.

03

Block Printing

Each stamp is pressed by hand with rhythmic precision. The artisan dips, aligns, and presses — turning metres of fabric into living art.

04

Wash & Finish

Fabrics are sun-dried, washed, and finished with care — revealing the final vibrant colours that only deepen with time.
